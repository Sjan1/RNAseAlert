Test 12: Fluorescein dilution serie

Jan Sklenar

09/06/2020

Fluorimeter instrument test with Fluorecein

For some time I have a suspiccion that our spectofluorimeter, Varioskan (Thermo) is not sensitive enough. We have to use 10ul in the assay, while published protocols suggest to use only 1ul. We measure 40 ul in 96 well plates instead of 20ul in 384 though. These difference do not intuitivelly acount for lack of a signal in low concentration range. For this reason, Fluorescein disodium salt to benchmark sensitivity is used here. The chemical is of purity > 80%, which should be sufficient for rough estimate of sensitivity.

Protocol:

1mM fluorescein disolved in water, further diluted to 100 times (5ul in 0.5ml) several times to low aM.

Dilution 1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8
concentration 1mM 10uM 100nM 1nM 100pM 1pM 10fM 100aM

Notes:

  • Fluorescein dilution serie
    High concentration:
    1mM = 1mmol /l = 1umol/ml = 1nmol/ul
    In absolute values:
    40ul => 40nmol
    Detection limit:
  • We can detect 100nM Fluorescein in larger volume (200ul) , which is 20pmol detected.
